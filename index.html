CelluWar

The Rules

CelluWar is a game based on Conway's Game of Life. Cells that have 2 or 3 neighbors survive (and remain the same color). Cells with fewer than 2 or more than 3 neighbors die due to under- or over-population, respectively. Empty locations (black) that have exactly 3 neighbors reproduce a daughter cell that is the color of the majority (red or blue) in that location's neighborhood.

The game has three steps:

  1. Your move: select a square to turn red.
  2. Computer's move: computer selects a square to turn blue.
  3. Conway's move: Conway's Game of Life is simulated (1 step).

The objective is to remove all the blue cells from the board, while at least one red cell remains alive. The game can only end after the Game of Life is Simulated.

Enable Hints

Toggle the switch on and off, to show hints. All squares that will be dead (empty) are marked with an "x" while squares that will be alive are marked with a "✓" in the corresponding color ("✓" or "✓").

Game Board

Make a move by clicking on a black or blue square.
