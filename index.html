Celluwar

The Rules

This is a game based on Conway's Game of Life. Cells that have 2 or 3 neighbors survive (and remain the same color). Cells with fewer than 2 or more than 3 neighbors die due to under- or over-population, respectively. Empty locations (black) that have exactly 3 neighbors reproduce a daughter cell that is the color of the majority (red or blue) in that location's neighborhood.

The game can only end after the Game of Life is Simulated.
